Quality Policy

The quality policy of Custom Printing Co. is to ensure customer satisfaction by providing quality printing solutions for digital and lithographic printing. We have identified the following core objectives in our business:

  • The enhancement of quality, specification, and supplier performance
  • Deliver customer satisfaction while minimizing risk
  • Maintain work ethics and best practices levels of service based on creating cost effective printing solutions for clients.
  • Ensure profitability and growth through sound financial management of the business.

We are committed to continuous improvement in the service to our customers and in all aspects of our business.
